Getting Started

October 2, 2019

Though our family has a website, its main purpose is not as a blog but as a newsletter and prayer list. I also set up another website this past week for our church here in Japan, Siloam Intl. Christian church–in Japanese, of course. Both sites are Github Pages and were built with Hugo using the Ananke theme. Through the experience of getting these two sites set up, I found that I really enjoyed writing the updates and that I am always tempted to write too much and off subject. So why not just start another little site?

At first my simple, little blog site was taken from “Make a Hugo Blog from Scratch”. I really liked it for its minimalistic style, but it lacked some features. That being the case, I have changed to the theme Pulp. It just works.
